About Me

Hi, I'm Lucas Ackman. I'm a video game engineer and designer. While I consider myself primary as someone who works in the development stages in the engine, I never turn down the opportunity to design gameplay when given the chance. At the moment, I currently use the Unity game engine with plans to migrate after graduating from university.

But who I am is a whole different story. Long story short: I grew up in a small town with dreams to bring children the stories and games I grew up with. Ultimately, I went down the videogame route because it is simply a medium through which I could also tell stories.

Since childhood I've always been really good with numbers, which naturally lead to being good with computers considering how often I spent my free time tinkering and trying to mod some of my favorite games. Over time, I learned the basics of what I was doing and wanted to learrn more in depth and I ended up at university where I realized just how much I didn't know yet.
